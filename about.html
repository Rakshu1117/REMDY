About Remedy

Redefining how India connects with trusted service professionals — reliable, fast, and transparent.

Our story: built for trust & excellence

Remedy started with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to skilled, verified professionals without the hassle of endless searching. Since 2021, we’ve built a platform where quality meets convenience — from emergency electrical repairs to precision electronics repair, mechanical help, construction, and delivery.

What makes us different? Every pro in our network undergoes a rigorous 5-step verification: application, document screening, background check, skill assessment, and final onboarding. This ensures that when you book a Remedy expert, you’re getting reliability and peace of mind.

Today, Remedy empowers thousands of independent professionals to grow their business with flexible schedules, fair commissions, and consistent job opportunities. We're not just a service platform — we're a community built on accountability, speed, and craftsmanship.
